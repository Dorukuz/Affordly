Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Affordly collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app and related pages on affordly.gatex.uk.

1. Information we collect

  • Budget data you enter (income, bills, wishes, amounts, currency preferences).
  • Subscription and purchase status from Apple and our subscription provider (RevenueCat).
  • Optional notification preferences if you enable Finn nudges.
  • Support correspondence if you email us.

2. How we use information

  • Provide and maintain app functionality (runway, goals, and guidance).
  • Process subscriptions and restore purchases.
  • Improve stability, performance, and product experience.
  • Respond to support and privacy requests.

3. Data storage and security

Your budget is stored on your device (SwiftData). If you use iCloud, data may sync to your private iCloud database (CloudKit) tied to your Apple ID. We use reasonable measures to protect data; no system is completely secure.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers strictly for app operations, billing (Apple, RevenueCat), or support.

5. Your choices

  • Reset or export data from Settings (CSV export available).
  • Manage notifications in Affordly Settings and iOS Settings.
  • Manage subscriptions in iOS Settings → Subscriptions.
  • Contact us for privacy questions (see below).

6. Children

Affordly is not directed to children under the age required by local law without parental involvement.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact app-care@gatex.uk.
