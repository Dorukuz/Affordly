Common topics

Subscriptions & Affordly Pro

Pro unlocks unlimited active wishes and side-by-side scenarios. Manage or cancel in Settings → Manage Subscription or via Apple Subscriptions. Use Restore Purchases on the paywall if you reinstalled the app.

iCloud sync

Budget data is local-first. When iCloud is enabled on your device, Affordly may sync to your private iCloud database. See our Privacy Policy.

Export or reset data

Export Data (CSV) and Reset Affordly on this device are in Settings.
